Welcome to the ChildrenGiving.org AI Simulation!

• To start, allow program camera access by clicking "Allow" when popup appears.

• To help the computer "learn", hold up at least 10 printed pictures of individual dogs, click on "Add Image of Dog" as you show each picture.

• Hold up at least 10 printed pictures of individual cats, click on "Add Image of Cat" as you show each picture.

• Hold up at least 10 printed pictures of miscellaneous items (like a house, person, or book), click on "Add Image of Neither" as you show each picture.



Interested? We are utilizing a TensorFlow model for this program. Interested in the code? Press control+shift+i to view it!
